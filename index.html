about us

MMD Community Care is a New Jersey DDD (Division of Developmental Disabilities) approved statewide provider. We provide a high standard of Community Support, Respite, and Individual Support to Adults with disabilities.

Our goal is to maintain your dignity by delivering excellent care to enable you to live safely and comfortably in your own home or while out in the community.

​

At MMD Community Care, we provide care for adults with special needs in a warm, friendly and comfortable atmosphere across the State of New Jersey. ​​

We pride our team on highly competent, compassionate, and dedicated professionals and strive to inspire each team member to reach for and achieve their full potential.
